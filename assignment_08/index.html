How to Build a Show Flow

An imperative part to a well organized show.

Collect all Available Data:

  • Event Agenda from the Client
  • Production Schedule from the Technical Director
  • Speaker Names and Timings from the Executive Producer
  • Desired "looks" from the Creative Director
  • Any videos, demos, furniture, etc from all


Sample Schedules



Populate information into one Excel Document:

  • Timings
  • Content
  • Screen Presets
  • Audio Needs
  • Lighting Cues


Sample Template


Sample Show Flow



The Complete Steps:

  1. Make your basic grid in excel.
  2. Enter all of the gathered information.
  3. Have a "paper tech" with the producer/client to confirm the information is accurate.
  4. Make any corrections.
  5. Distribute to crew.
  6. Have a "cue to cue" with your running crew.
  7. Rehearse with talent/client.
  8. Make any corrections.
  9. Call Show!
  10. Give "final" cue sheet to the producer.
  11. Celebrate a successful show!!
  12. Rinse and Repeat.
