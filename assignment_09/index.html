The 80's

The 1980's were both awesome and horrible at the same time.
A decade of contradiction. If you lived through them, you know what I mean.
Everything was neon. Fashion was both baggy and tight. Color combinations were tottaly tubular.
Music was over-digitized, yet completely rad. Big ballads and dance music shared the radio waves and competed on MTV.
The Cold War ended. The Berlin Wall fell. But, Jazzercize was the place to be.
