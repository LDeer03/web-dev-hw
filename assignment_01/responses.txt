How is the web different than the internet?
The Web is the information found on the Internet. You need the global network of Internet networks to access the information which is the Web. Another way to look at this difference is; the Internet is infrastructure while the Web is service on top of that infrastructure.

Briefly describe how the internet works.
It distributes packets of information along a network of routers. It does this through a wire buried in the ground. Client, to Server/ISP, Router, Server/ISP, to Client.

What is a directory, and what is its relationship to files?
Directories are a file management system. Directories are done in a hierarchical naming format. Folders and Directories are essentially the same thing and files nest within.

Why is using a VCS beneficial?
A version control system, or VCS, is very beneficial when it comes to viewing the history, collaborating with coworkers, and knowing when what was done to what.
