Ham & Cheese Sliders

An easy crowd pleaser that will bring the comfort of home and the ease of summer.

Ingredients for Sandwiches

  • 24 dinner rolls or Hawaiian Rolls
  • 24 pieces honey ham
  • 24 small slices Swiss cheese
  • 1/3 c. mayonnaise
  • 1/3 c. miracle whip

Ingredients for Poppy Seed Sauce

  • 1 t. poppy seeds
  • 1.5 T. yellow mustard
  • 1/2 c. melted butter
  • 1 T. minced onion or shallot
  • 1/2 t. Worteshire sauce

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise and miracle whip.
  2. Spread onto both sides of the center of each roll.
  3. Place a slice of ham and a slice of Swiss inside of each roll.
  4. Close rolls and place them very close together into a large baking dish or heavy cookie sheet. Set aside
  5. In a medium bowl, whisk together all of the poppy seed sauce ingredients.
  6. Pour evenly over all of the sandwiches. You do not have to use all of the sauce! Just use enough to cover the tops. Let sit 10 minutes or until butter sets slightly. Cover with foil.
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 12-15 min. or until cheese is melted. Uncover and cook for 2 additional min. or until tops are slightly brown and crispy.
  8. Serve warm
